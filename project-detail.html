The Challenge

Users were finding it difficult to navigate the existing property booking process. The conversion rate was dropping, and user feedback highlighted confusion during the final payment stages.

The goal was to redesign the mobile application to simplify the booking flow, build trust through clear pricing, and enhance the overall visual aesthetic to feel more premium.

The Solution

I led the redesign focusing on a "one task per screen" approach for the checkout flow. We introduced a persistent price breakdown, clearer photography guidelines for hosts, and a refined dark-mode UI to reduce eye strain for nighttime browsing.

Results

Post-launch metrics showed a 24% increase in booking completions and a significant reduction in customer support tickets related to payment confusion. The new design language was subsequently adopted across the web platform as well.
